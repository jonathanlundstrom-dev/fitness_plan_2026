OVERLOAD RULES

Compound Lifts
Add 5 lbs when you complete all reps in all sets for 2 consecutive sessions with good form.
Pull-Ups
+1 rep per session target. Add weight vest or dumbbell once you hit 12+ reps consistently.
Long Run
Add 0.5 miles every 2 weeks. Keep effort conversational — if you can't speak, slow down.
Cardio Intensity
Increase speed or resistance by 5% every 3 weeks on interval sessions.
Deload Weeks
Weeks 4, 8, and 12 — cut all volume by 50%. This is where the gains are cemented.
